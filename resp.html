  1. Remarkable Purple Cow

    Oktober 20th, 2012

    When starting a company in any field, chances are that there are already companies around offering similar kind of product or service.

    So the question becomes how to position yourself, what to produce and importantly, how to get people to care ?

    The guru

    While thinking about these questions for my company I stumbled upon a marketing guru named Seth Godin.

    Seth Godin has built a few successful businesses and made many popular books such as The Purple Cow, Tribes and Linchpin.

    He explains how the old model of companies interrupting people with ads is not working anymore. There are so many choices today that people have become experts in ignoring stuff that is not personally relevant.

    The internet gives us all a way to put things into the open. It brings a great opportunity but it also means more crap to sort out in our daily lives.

    Seth claims that the best way to get attention today is by being remarkable, to be a purple cow. Read on.

    Having a story

    People think remarkable means to be great but re-markable means something worth talking about. Seth Godin

    The challenge is to do something that people choose to talk about. Something different, something that sets you apart from what is considered average.

    Hipmunk did this by building a flight search that sorts results by flights that suck less. They realize that flying is not always fun so sorting by “agony” is very useful and people love it.

    TOMS is a shoe company that gives shoes to a poor child for every pair of shoes they sell. So for every pair you buy, you help a child in need.

    These are stories that people talk about, they go deeper than just doing ad campaigns and they are the best marketing you can ever get.

    “Nobody stops their car to watch a brown cow, but if it is purple people would stop” Seth Godin

    Its not difficult to get your word out, the thing that is difficult is to get people to care Seth Godin
